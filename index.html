About Me

I work in Tech at TOUCH Community Services, a non-profit organization where technology directly supports meaningful work. My role is wonderfully varied — one day I'm building Power BI dashboards, the next I'm developing OutSystems applications or working on AI solutions.

I studied Computer Science with a focus on Data Analytics, which gave me solid foundations in machine learning, Python, and various programming languages. While I don't code daily anymore, that knowledge shapes how I approach problems and evaluate solutions.

Honestly? I'm still figuring out exactly where I want to specialize. What I know is that I'm genuinely curious about AI, cloud technologies, and building things that make work easier. I'm drawn to the practical side of tech — tools and systems that people actually use and benefit from.

I believe good technology should feel invisible — it just works, helps people do their jobs better, and doesn't get in the way.
