01 — About

Who I am

I'm Winfield — a tech generalist working in Digital Solutions, Data & AI at TOUCH Community Services in Singapore. My work spans a broad range: from keeping systems running and supporting teams, to building software solutions, making sense of data, and exploring what AI can do in a social services context.

I'm largely self-taught, and I've come to embrace the wide scope rather than fight it. Every new problem is a chance to learn something — and the best solutions often come from connecting dots across disciplines. The field moves fast, and honestly, I wouldn't have it any other way.

Outside of work, you'll find me tinkering with side projects, self-hosting tools, or picking up something new just for the fun of it. Same curiosity, fewer meetings.

At the end of the day, what drives me is simple: I want the technology I build to actually help — especially for the people doing the hardest, most meaningful work in our communities.

Curious by nature

Always learning — whether it's a new framework, a better process, or a different way of thinking about a problem.

Impact-driven

Success isn't measured in lines of code, but in whether the work genuinely helps someone do their job better.

Based in Singapore

Working in the social service sector, building tools that matter for Singapore's communities.

Builder by habit

If there's a problem worth solving, I'd rather build something than wait for a solution to appear.
